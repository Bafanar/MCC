Select Your Zoom Connection Method

Please choose how you are connecting to Zoom:

Instructions for Mobile Phone

  1. Join the Zoom meeting using the Zoom mobile app.
  2. Tap the "More" button (three dots) in the bottom-right corner.
  3. Select "Language Interpretation" from the menu.
  4. Choose your preferred language from the list.
  5. Tap "Done" to confirm your selection.

Instructions for Laptop

  1. Join the Zoom meeting using the Zoom desktop application.
  2. Click the "Interpretation" button in the meeting controls.
  3. Select your preferred language from the list of available options.
  4. Click "OK" to confirm your selection.
