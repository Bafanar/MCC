Zoom connection Instructions

Instructions on how to connect to a Zoom meeting

Please choose how you are connecting to Zoom:

Instructions for Mobile Phone

  1. Download the Zoom app from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android).
  2. Open the Zoom app.
  3. Tap "Join a Meeting".
  4. Enter the Meeting ID and your name.


  5. Tap "Join" and enter the meeting passcode if prompted.

Instructions for Laptop

  1. Open your web browser and go to https://zoom.us/join.
  2. Enter the Meeting ID and your name.


  3. Click "Join"
  4. If prompted, download and install the Zoom application.
  5. Once installed, the meeting will launch automatically.
  6. Enter the meeting passcode if required.
